HackBeanpot 2021 Concept Development

Defining HackBeanpot's brand and exploring hackathon themes
Role:Social & Outreach Designer
Project: HackBeanpot 2021
Tools: Figma, Google Drive, Slack, Trello, Zoom
Location: Remote
Duration: April 2020 - March 2021

Problem

No brand guidelines, different interpretations of HackBeanpot, and no theme.

As a collective, we needed to come up with a hackathon theme that focuses on promoting positive learning experiences in the Tech space without excluding fields like Design or Project Management. We also needed to consider visualizing our event for the entire hackathon community.

Challenges

Since this was my first time contributing to a long-term hackathon event management project, I never had to think about the scope beyond the hacker experience before. Additionally, this is the first time HackBean Core had to organize a virtual hackathon in a completely virtual space during a pandemic.

Before the Design team could start brainstorming theme ideas for HackBeanpot 2021, we had to first run through our brand strategy and our "bean voice" (our brand voice).

Brand Development Objectives

  • Meet with core to talk through personal perspectives about HackBeanpot
  • Define HackBeanpot's persona and set brand voice guidelines
  • Develop ideal Personas for Hackers, Collaborators, Judges, and Sponsors

Design Team Objectives

  • Participate in brainstorming sessions and feedback activities using Zoom, Trello, and Figma.
  • Create a design system that's on brand with HackBeanpot's character and theme
  • Include a set of design components to reuse throughout the year
